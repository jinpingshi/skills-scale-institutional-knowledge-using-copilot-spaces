# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers orchestrate release planning, execution, communications, and incident response. They ensure release criteria and checklists are followed to deliver reliable deployments to production.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and coordinate release schedules and deployment windows
- Ensure pre-release checklists and criteria are met
- Manage release communications to stakeholders and support teams
- Coordinate incident response and rollback procedures when needed
- Maintain release documentation and post-deployment verification

### Goals
- Deliver smooth, reliable releases with minimal disruption
- Reduce deployment risk through standardized processes
- Maintain clear communication throughout the release cycle
- Ensure quick recovery when issues arise

### Typical Communication
- Release schedules and deployment notifications
- Pre-release readiness reviews with PM, PdM, and QA Lead
- Post-release status updates and incident reports
- Coordination with Developers and QA on deployment verification

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads define and oversee the test strategy, manage test resources, coordinate bug triage, and report on product quality. They ensure comprehensive test coverage and quality gates are met throughout the delivery process.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain the overall test strategy and approach
- Coordinate test planning and resource allocation
- Lead bug triage sessions and prioritization
- Report on quality metrics and test coverage
- Ensure acceptance criteria are testable and validated
- Establish quality gates for each project phase

### Goals
- Maintain high product quality and test coverage
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Provide clear visibility into product quality status
- Enable rapid, confident releases through automation

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and quality reports
- Bug triage meetings with Developers and PM
- Quality gate reviews before releases
- Collaboration with Developers on acceptance criteria and test automation

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts gather, refine, and document requirements, bridging gaps between business stakeholders and delivery teams. They translate business goals into clear, actionable project specifications.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze and refine requirements for clarity and feasibility
- Create process flows, use cases, and functional specifications
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs
- Facilitate requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Maintain traceability between business goals and deliverables

### Goals
- Ensure delivery teams have clear, complete requirements
- Minimize rework due to misunderstood requirements
- Bridge communication gaps between business and technical teams
- Validate that solutions deliver intended business value

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documents and user stories
- Stakeholder interviews and validation sessions
- Collaboration with PM and PdM on prioritization
- Clarification sessions with Developers on implementation details

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers design and validate user experiences, create wireframes and prototypes, and champion usability throughout development. They ensure products are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define user flows and interaction patterns
- Establish and maintain design systems and UI guidelines
- Validate designs with users and stakeholders
- Collaborate with Developers on implementation feasibility

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-centered product experiences
- Ensure consistency across the product interface
- Validate designs through user feedback and testing
- Balance user needs with technical constraints and business goals

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Collaboration with Product Manager on user needs and priorities
- Implementation handoffs and design specs for Developers
- Usability testing results and recommendations
